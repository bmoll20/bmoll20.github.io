# Ben Moll

Hi my name is Ben Moll! This is a website that I use to host information about my research and projects that I have worked on. I have an M.S. in Earth and Atmospheric Science with a specialization in Meteorology/Climatology from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. I also hold a B.S. in Physics with minors in Computer Science and Mathematics from Concordia University-Nebraska. Thanks for taking the time to look through my work, feel free to reach out!

This site showcases my:
- Research
- Modeling projects
- Applied data science work
